This directory contains a subset of images of
[Market-1501](http://www.liangzheng.org/Project/project_reid.html). The
files are indexed in `index.p` together with joint annotations obtained with
[OpenPose](https://github.com/CMU-Perceptual-Computing-Lab/openpose). It is
shared to reproduce results of [A Variational U-Net for Conditional
Appearance and Shape Generation](https://compvis.github.io/vunet/).
